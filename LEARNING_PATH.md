# Learning Path

Use this as the workbook route from broad categories to 细分赛道.

## 1. Learn The Big Buckets

- **Historical movements:** Art Nouveau, Art Deco, Bauhaus, Constructivism, Pop Art. These are strong because they imply era, composition, palette, and ornament.
- **Graphic systems:** Swiss International, Brutalist Graphic Design, Risograph, Punk DIY, Luxury Editorial. These are strongest for posters, slides, covers, and brand systems.
- **Interface styles:** Material Design, Glassmorphism, Neumorphism, Bento UI, Frutiger Aero, Neo-Brutalist UI. These are strongest for app and website prompts.
- **Speculative worlds:** Cyberpunk, Solarpunk, Steampunk, Cassette Futurism, Afrofuturism. These are strongest for environments, products, and cinematic concepts.
- **Cultural/worldbuilding lanes:** Viking Norse, Gothic Cathedral, Islamic Geometric, Wabi-Sabi, Dark Academia. These are strongest for environments, games, packaging, and themed brands.
- **Material/interior lanes:** Brutalist Concrete, Japandi, Organic Modern, Holographic Packaging. These are strongest when surfaces matter.
- **Photo/cinema grades:** Film Noir, Golden Hour Editorial, VHS Camcorder, Analog Horror. These are strongest when the output should feel photographed or filmed.
- **Illustration/game render modes:** Pixel Art, Manga Screentone, Ligne Claire, Woodcut, Claymation. These are strongest when the medium itself is the style.
- **Lifestyle aesthetics:** Streetwear Drop, Cottagecore, Old Money, Indie Sleaze. These are strongest for campaigns, fashion, social visuals, and moodboards.

## 3. Learn Style Ingredients

When a style is working, identify which ingredients caused it:

- **Palette:** Cyberpunk is often magenta/cyan/black; Art Deco is often black/gold/emerald/ivory.
- **Shape:** Bauhaus uses simple geometry; Art Nouveau uses flowing botanical curves.
- **Material:** Brutalist Concrete is raw concrete; Steampunk is brass, leather, steam, and gauges.
- **Composition:** Swiss International is grid-first; Baroque is diagonal and dramatic.
- **Texture:** Risograph has misregistered ink grain; VHS has scanline noise.
- **Era signal:** Frutiger Aero feels early-2000s; Mid-Century Modern feels postwar domestic modernity.

## 4. Build Hybrids

Hybrid prompts are useful once you know the ingredients:

```text
Create {{SUBJECT}} as Cyberpunk x Swiss International: neon night atmosphere and reflective surfaces, but strict grid layout, spare typography, and disciplined information hierarchy.
```

```text
Create {{SUBJECT}} as Wabi-Sabi x Tech Minimal Hardware: imperfect natural surfaces, quiet asymmetry, precise aluminum details, warm neutral palette, and sparse product lighting.
```

## 5. Keep A Personal Vocabulary

When you see a style you like, save three things:

- the style name
- three visual markers
- one negative instruction

Example:

```text
Neo-Brutalism: thick black borders, loud flat colors, offset shadows. Avoid glassmorphism and subtle gradients.
```
